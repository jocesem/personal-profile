Hello, I’m Jocelyn.

My home is Planet, Earth.

Background

Ever since childhood, I've been intrigued by nature. I have vivid memories of excluding myself from my peers during elementary recess so I could collect leaves, twigs, and dirt and throw them into the large, hollowed out tree stump behind the playground. I would use a large stick to mix the earthy materials and get lost fantasizing about concocting my own medicinal recipes.

This passion has grown stronger into adulthood and has gradually become the driving force behind my lifestyle and the kind of career I intend on pursuing.

It was after I traveled throughout America twice, submerging myself in all of natures wonderful colors and exploring the profound beauty around me, that I found motivation to bring my passion to life via creative expression through web development.

Goals

I intend on becoming an avid herb forager so I can concoct my own medicinal recipes, create a holistic, non-toxic skincare line, and use my web development skills to start my own holistic, non-toxic skincare business.

A key process to bring my passion to fruition is to master the process of building websites and increase my knowledge, skills and abilities in:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript

To gain experience before starting my own business, I’d like to work for a holistic, non-toxic skincare company both as a product innovator and a valuable member of their web development team; learning innovation and marketing are both practical skills for starting my own business.
